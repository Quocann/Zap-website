🎯 What is Grid Wick Strategy?

Grid Wick Strategy uses candlestick wicks to identify Support and Resistance levels. The key rule: Wick ≥ 2x Body means that price respected this level!

📐 The Formula

Wick ≥ 2x Body AND Wick ≥ 40% of Range
  • Upper Wick ≥ 2x Body = Resistance (sellers dominated)
  • Lower Wick ≥ 2x Body = Support (buyers dominated)
  • Wick ≥ 40% of Range = Strong level, not just noise

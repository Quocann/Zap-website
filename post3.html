👥 Multi-Agent Collaboration

📅 April 3, 2026 | 📂 AI Agents

🤖 What is Multi-Agent System?

Instead of one AI doing everything, I created a team of specialized agents that work together - just like a real company!

👥 My AI Team

  • 🤖 Trading Agent: Analyzes market, suggests trades (bullish & active)
  • 📈 Research Agent: Provides data, counter-arguments, risk assessment (skeptical)
  • 📊 Data Agent: Live trading stats, portfolio numbers (factual)
  • 🔔 Alert Agent: Sends notifications to Discord/Telegram

💬 How They Discuss

Example Discussion:

🤖 Trading: "RIVER has BUY signal, UP trend + near support"
📈 Research: "But last 3 trades lost - check M15 first"
📊 Data: "Current balance $40, 0 trades today"
🤖 Trading: "Agreed - wait for clearer signal"
📈 Research: "Good decision. Let's monitor."

🎯 Why It Works

  • Different perspectives: Each agent has different bias
  • Check & balance: Prevents reckless decisions
  • Continuous discussion: 24/7 team meeting
  • Better decisions: Group think vs individual

🔮 Future Plans

Adding more specialized agents: Sentiment Agent, News Agent, Portfolio Manager Agent!
